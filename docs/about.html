As you can see I am a huge fan of 'Rick and Morty', 'Gravity Falls', and cartoons in general. I am also a mental health advocate, and feel mental health is an important topic in our life.

Why Share?

It feels odd just placing my artwork out there where anyone could do anything with it without my knowledge. But when I critically thought about it. That is what every developer does when they create a pallette, a library, a framework, or anything else and put it up on any shareable service for everyone with a MIT, GPL v.2, or GPL v.3 among other licenses similar to these. So I felt it was my turn. Also, with that we face the chance of critism, which (is changing for the better) when we share and upon receiving can turn us off from producing because were afraid to ask questions that hold us back from completing projects. Be bold, be strong, and be kind.
